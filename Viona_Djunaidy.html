Viona Djunaidy

Role: Frontend Developer

Viona Djunaidy is a computer science major eager to step out of her comfort zone to gain new experiences and contribute her skills to an organization. She aims to develop her organizational, communication, creative-thinking, and teamwork abilities. Viona possesses strong soft skills, including initiative, communication, open-mindedness, problem-solving, and teamwork, alongside hard skills in scriptwriting, storyboarding, basic video editing, and project management. Her organizational experience includes serving as treasurer for a school play, participating in community service with Habitat for Humanity, and being an active member of Robogals Binus and the Modeling Club. Viona has also received recognition for her contributions, including the “Best Volunteer” award and the 2nd place in the Director's Choice Award at her high school visual arts exhibition. Currently, she is a Freshmen Leader and Partner, guiding new students through their first-year program.
